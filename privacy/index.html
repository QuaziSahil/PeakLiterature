Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Welcome to PeakLiterature. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Reading Progress: We store your reading and listening progress locally in your browser.
  • Favorites: Your favorite books are saved in your browser's local storage.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and for analytics.
  • Log Data: Our servers may log information including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • LibriVox: Audiobook content is sourced from LibriVox, a public domain collection.
  • Project Gutenberg: Ebook content is sourced from Project Gutenberg.
  • Internet Archive: Additional content is sourced from the Internet Archive.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality (saving progress, favorites)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use our site
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to display relevant ads

Data Storage

PeakLiterature stores your reading progress and favorites locally in your browser using localStorage. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local browser data at any time
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Use browser privacy modes

Children's Privacy

Our site contains public domain literature suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
